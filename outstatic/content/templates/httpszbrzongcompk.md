# Standard Operating Procedure: Zong Business Resource (ZBR) Portal Management

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the professional methodology for managing corporate telecommunications services via the Zong Business Resource (ZBR) portal. The ZBR platform serves as the centralized dashboard for enterprise account administrators to monitor usage, manage subscriptions, oversee bulk billing, and request service modifications. Adherence to this protocol ensures data accuracy, cost optimization, and operational continuity for organizational mobile assets.

## Phase 1: Access and Security Protocols
*   **Authentication:** Navigate to [https://zbr.zong.com.pk/](https://zbr.zong.com.pk/). Ensure you are using a secure, private network or a managed VPN.
*   **Credential Management:** Input authorized corporate credentials. Do not save passwords in browser caches on shared workstations.
*   **MFA Verification:** Complete Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) via the registered administrator mobile number if prompted.
*   **Session Security:** Always log out explicitly via the 'Logout' button after the session concludes to prevent unauthorized persistence.

## Phase 2: Billing and Financial Reconciliation
*   **Invoice Retrieval:** Navigate to the 'Billing' tab to access current and historical monthly invoices.
*   **Data Verification:** Cross-reference the total billing amount against internal cost center allocations.
*   **Payment Processing:** If utilizing the portal for direct payments, verify the transaction reference number against the corporate treasury ledger.
*   **PDF Archival:** Download all invoices in PDF format for the internal audit repository.

## Phase 3: Subscription and Fleet Management
*   **Active Subscriber Audit:** Review the list of assigned MSISDNs (mobile numbers) to identify inactive or terminated employees.
*   **Service Modification:** Select specific lines to apply 'Value Added Services' (VAS) or adjust data packages based on consumption patterns.
*   **Disconnection Protocol:** Flag lines belonging to departed staff for immediate suspension to prevent unauthorized usage and incurrence of unnecessary costs.
*   **Bulk Actions:** Utilize the 'Bulk Upload' feature for changes affecting more than five lines to ensure uniform data application.

## Phase 4: Usage Monitoring and Reporting
*   **Analytics Review:** Access the 'Usage Reports' section to monitor high-data consumption patterns.
*   **Threshold Alerts:** Configure automated alerts for lines that exceed 80% of their allocated monthly data limit.
*   **Trend Analysis:** Export quarterly CSV reports to analyze departmental expenditure versus productivity metrics.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls
*   **Pro Tip:** Use the 'Scheduled Reports' feature to have monthly usage summaries emailed to your finance department automatically.
*   **Pro Tip:** Always run a 'Trial Run' on a single line before executing a mass-change (e.g., package migration) for the entire fleet.
*   **Pitfall:** Ignoring the 'Pending Requests' dashboard. Always verify that previous requests have reached a 'Completed' status before initiating new changes.
*   **Pitfall:** Failing to rotate the administrative password following any personnel changes in the IT or Finance department.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: I have forgotten my administrator credentials; how can I reset them?**
A: Use the 'Forgot Password' link on the login screen. If access remains restricted, you must contact your assigned Zong Corporate Account Manager (CAM) to request a credential reset for security verification.

**Q: Why are my changes not reflecting immediately on the handset?**
A: Most plan or service changes require a synchronization window of 24–48 hours. If changes are not reflected after 48 hours, perform a device restart; if the issue persists, log a support ticket via the portal.

**Q: Can I manage multiple corporate sub-accounts under one ZBR login?**
A: Yes, provided your Zong account manager has mapped all sub-accounts under a single master administrator profile. Navigate to the 'Account Switcher' dropdown in the top navigation bar to toggle between entities.
