# Standard Operating Procedure: Daily Instagram Quote Content Creation

This SOP establishes a standardized workflow for the daily creation and dissemination of motivational or industry-relevant quote posts on Instagram. The objective is to maintain brand consistency, increase audience engagement, and ensure a streamlined production cycle that avoids last-minute content fatigue. By adhering to this process, the social media team will ensure that daily assets are high-quality, on-brand, and optimized for maximum reach.

## Phase 1: Research and Content Curation
*   **Source Verification:** Utilize curated databases (e.g., Notion, Pinterest boards, or saved folders) to pull pre-approved quotes.
*   **Relevance Audit:** Review the selected quote against the current day’s brand focus or audience pain points.
*   **Copyright Compliance:** Ensure all quotes are either public domain or properly attributed to the original author.
*   **Thematic Alignment:** Verify the quote matches the current week’s visual aesthetic or content pillar.

## Phase 2: Design and Asset Production
*   **Template Selection:** Open the master brand template file in Canva/Adobe Express to maintain consistent font and color palettes.
*   **Typography Check:** Ensure text is centered, legible, and adheres to the brand’s hierarchy (e.g., primary quote font vs. attribution font).
*   **Asset Export:** Save the file in the appropriate format (1080x1350 for portrait/4:5 aspect ratio) for maximum screen real estate.
*   **Visual QC:** Double-check for typos, punctuation errors, and proper spacing before final export.

## Phase 3: Copywriting and Engagement Setup
*   **Caption Drafting:** Write a caption that adds value or invites conversation. Include a specific Call-to-Action (CTA), such as "Save this for later" or "Tag a friend who needs to hear this."
*   **Hashtag Strategy:** Select a mix of 3-5 broad tags (e.g., #motivation) and 2-3 niche, industry-specific tags to reach the target demographic.
*   **Alt-Text Entry:** Populate the "Accessibility" field with a detailed description of the quote for screen readers.
*   **Tagging:** If applicable, tag the original author or any featured influencers.

## Phase 4: Final Publishing and Review
*   **Scheduling:** Load the asset into the social media management tool (e.g., Buffer, Later, or Meta Business Suite).
*   **Draft Review:** Perform a final preview on a mobile device to ensure the layout looks crisp on a phone screen.
*   **Engagement Monitoring:** Post the content and commit to responding to the first 5-10 comments within the first 60 minutes of publication.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls
*   **Pro Tip:** Batch process your quotes on a weekly basis (e.g., create 7 quotes on Friday) rather than daily to prevent burnout and ensure a cohesive aesthetic.
*   **Pro Tip:** Use the "Save" metric rather than "Likes" as your primary KPI; high save counts indicate that your content is truly valuable to your audience.
*   **Pitfall:** Over-complicating designs. Keep the quote the hero; avoid cluttered backgrounds that make text hard to read.
*   **Pitfall:** Ignoring comments. Posting without engaging kills the algorithm’s reach; always foster a community conversation.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Should I use a different color scheme for every post?**
A: No. Consistency is key to brand recognition. Stick to your defined brand color palette for all quote assets to ensure your grid remains cohesive.

**Q: What time should I post these quotes?**
A: Review your Instagram Insights. Identify the window when your audience is most active and schedule your quotes to go live 30 minutes before that peak period to build momentum.

**Q: Can I use AI to generate quotes?**
A: Yes, AI is a great tool for brainstorming; however, always manually review the output for "hallucinated" quotes or incorrect attributions to protect your brand’s credibility.
