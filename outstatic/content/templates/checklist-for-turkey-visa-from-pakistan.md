# Standard Operating Procedure: Turkey E-Visa and Sticker Visa Application for Pakistani Nationals

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the mandatory requirements and procedural steps for Pakistani citizens applying for a Turkish visa. Please note that Turkish visa policies for Pakistani nationals are subject to change based on residency status and passport type. Generally, Pakistani ordinary passport holders are required to apply for a sticker visa through designated service providers (such as Gerry’s Visa), while holders of valid Schengen, USA, UK, or Ireland visas or residence permits may be eligible for an E-Visa. Always verify your eligibility on the official [Turkish Ministry of Foreign Affairs portal](https://www.evisa.gov.tr/) before beginning your application.

## 1. Documentation Preparation Checklist

Before initiating the online application or visiting the visa center, ensure all documents are original, translated (if required), and notarized where necessary.

### Personal & Travel Documents
*   **Passport:** Original, valid for at least 6 months beyond your planned date of departure, with at least two blank pages.
*   **Copies:** Clear photocopies of the passport bio-data page and previous visas/entry stamps.
*   **Application Form:** Completed and signed visa application form (available via the official Pre-Application System).
*   **Photographs:** Two biometric photos (5x5 cm), white background, taken within the last 6 months.
*   **Travel Insurance:** Comprehensive travel health insurance covering the entire duration of stay (minimum €30,000 coverage).
*   **Flight Reservation:** Confirmed round-trip flight booking (do not purchase non-refundable tickets until the visa is granted).
*   **Hotel Reservation:** Proof of accommodation (hotel booking confirmation) matching the travel dates.

### Financial & Employment Proof
*   **Bank Statements:** Last 6 months of personal bank statements, signed and stamped by the issuing bank.
*   **Account Maintenance Certificate:** An official letter from the bank confirming the account holder's details and balance.
*   **Employment Proof (for employees):** Salary slips for the last 3 months, a "No Objection Certificate" (NOC) from the employer, and a copy of the company’s NTN/Registration certificate.
*   **Business Proof (for business owners):** Business Registration Certificate (FBR/SEC), Chamber of Commerce membership, and business bank statements.

## 2. The Application Process

*   **Step 1: Determine Visa Type:** Identify if you require an E-Visa (Conditional) or a Sticker Visa based on your current visa status (Schengen, UK, USA).
*   **Step 2: Pre-Application:** Access the [Visa Pre-Application System](https://www.visa.gov.tr/) to fill out the form and upload digital copies of your documents.
*   **Step 3: Appointment Booking:** Once the pre-application is submitted, schedule an appointment at your nearest Gerry’s Visa (VFS Global or authorized service center) in Pakistan.
*   **Step 4: Fee Submission:** Pay the visa processing fee and service charges at the visa center during your appointment. 
*   **Step 5: Submission & Biometrics:** Attend the appointment in person to submit original documents and provide biometric data (fingerprints).
*   **Step 6: Tracking:** Use the reference number provided on your receipt to track the application status online.
*   **Step 7: Passport Collection:** Collect your passport from the center once you receive notification that the processing is complete.

## 3. Pro Tips & Pitfalls

*   **Pro Tip:** Always carry a printed copy of your Travel Insurance policy document; many applicants fail at the desk because their insurance is not printed.
*   **Pro Tip:** If you are a freelancer, provide proof of consistent monthly income through invoices or digital transaction history to establish financial credibility.
*   **Pitfall - Inconsistent Data:** Ensure your hotel and flight booking dates align perfectly with the dates mentioned in your cover letter and visa application form. Discrepancies are a common cause for rejection.
*   **Pitfall - Last Minute Submission:** Never apply within 15 days of your planned departure. Processing times can vary, and peak travel seasons (summer/winter) often lead to significant backlogs.

## 4. Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Can I apply for a Turkey visa if I do not have a valid Schengen, US, or UK visa?**
A: Yes, you can apply for a Sticker Visa. You will need to submit your application through the Turkish Embassy or Consulate-authorized visa centers (Gerry’s) in Pakistan by providing full supporting documentation.

**Q: Is the visa fee refundable if my application is rejected?**
A: No, the visa application fee is a processing fee and is non-refundable regardless of the outcome of your application.

**Q: How long does the visa processing typically take?**
A: Standard processing times usually range between 15 to 30 working days. However, it is highly recommended to apply at least 4-6 weeks before your intended travel date to account for potential administrative delays.
