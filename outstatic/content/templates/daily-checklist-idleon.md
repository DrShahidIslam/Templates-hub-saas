# Standard Operating Procedure: Daily Maintenance Routine for IdleOn

This SOP outlines the standardized daily workflow for maximizing account progression and resource efficiency in *Legends of Idleon*. Consistent execution of these tasks ensures optimal AFK gains, continuous production, and steady account-wide growth. By following this systematic approach, you minimize downtime and ensure all time-gated mechanics remain synchronized with your progression goals.

## Phase 1: Global Resource Collection
*   **Idleon Shop:** Visit every town shop. Purchase all available bags, inventory expansions, and rare materials (e.g., Pens, Water, Golden Food) to maximize future crafting potential.
*   **Daily Gifts:** Collect the free daily gift from the Gem Shop menu.
*   **Daily Bosses:** Run your maximum allotted keys for bosses (e.g., Amokus, Dr. Defecaus, Chizoar). Use multi-kill mechanics if available to boost drop rates.
*   **Colosseum:** Utilize daily Colosseum tickets. Focus on the highest tier currently accessible to maximize EXP and Statuette drops.

## Phase 2: Production and Processing
*   **Alchemy:** Check the Alchemy Cauldrons. Collect bubbles, perform "Liquid" upgrades, and ensure all characters are assigned to active research slots.
*   **Stamps & Shrines:** Deposit excess resources into the Stamp library to maximize stat bonuses. Check active Shrine positions—ensure they are placed on maps with high-density spawns if applicable.
*   **Refinery (World 3):** Cycle the Refinery. Promote ranks when cycles hit 100% and ensure the output is configured for the most critical resource tier.
*   **Trapping & Breeding (World 4):** Reset traps and collect critters. Ensure your "Shiny" breeding progress is active and that the fence yard is optimized for high-tier pet generation.
*   **Cooking:** Collect gathered meals and assign new recipes or upgrade existing ones based on current growth requirements.

## Phase 3: Character Management & AFK Setup
*   **Talent Refresh:** Check for any new skill points gained from leveling and allocate them to AFK efficiency or damage modifiers.
*   **Printer Sampling (World 3):** If you have leveled up a skill significantly, take a new 3D Printer sample to maximize your hourly material output.
*   **Inventory Audit:** Clear out junk items. Move materials to the Storage Chest and sell gear that is obsolete for your current level bracket.
*   **AFK Assignment:** Finalize the daily location for every character. Ensure their active skills match their current task (e.g., "Active AFK" vs "Mining/Chopping"). Verify that inventory space is sufficient for the duration of the AFK window.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls
*   **Pitfall - The "AFK Trap":** Never leave a character in a zone where their inventory fills up too quickly. If your bags fill, AFK efficiency drops to 0%. Always calculate your inventory capacity against the drop rate of the map.
*   **Pro Tip - Talent Presets:** Use your Talent Presets effectively. Maintain one for "Combat/Damage" and one for "Skilling/Efficiency." Swapping these daily before and after tasks can increase your yields by 20% or more.
*   **Pro Tip - World 3 Construction:** Always prioritize the "NBLB" (No Bubble Left Behind) talent in Alchemy. It is the single most effective way to level high-tier bubbles without massive resource investment.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Should I use my daily boss keys immediately?**
A: Yes, unless you are waiting for a specific drop-rate buff (like a lucky lad card or higher character level). Hoarding keys serves no purpose in the long run; use them daily to accumulate boss-specific crafting materials.

**Q: How do I handle inventory management for long AFK sessions?**
A: Utilize the "Auto-Loot" feature from the Gem Shop if possible. If free-to-play, focus on upgrading bags through shop purchases and prioritizing "Carry Capacity" stamps to extend your AFK time.

**Q: Does it matter which character collects the daily shop items?**
A: No, the shop inventory is account-wide. However, it is most efficient to use your main character or the one with the highest carry capacity to minimize trips to the storage chest.
