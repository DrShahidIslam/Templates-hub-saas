# Standard Operating Procedure: EPF e-Nomination Process

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the mandatory steps for Employees’ Provident Fund (EPF) members to complete their e-Nomination through the Unified Member Portal. Filing an e-Nomination is a critical compliance requirement that ensures social security benefits are transferred seamlessly to the designated nominee in the event of the member’s demise, avoiding complex legal disputes and delays. All active EPF members are advised to ensure their profile data is updated and their UAN is linked with an active Aadhaar and mobile number before initiating this process.

## Phase 1: Pre-requisites and Preparation
*   **Verify UAN Activation:** Ensure the Universal Account Number (UAN) is active and the member is registered on the UAN Member Portal.
*   **Aadhaar Linking:** The UAN must be seeded with a verified Aadhaar number.
*   **Profile Completion:** Ensure the member profile (Photo, Date of Birth, Gender, Marital Status, and Permanent Address) is 100% complete.
*   **Mobile Connectivity:** Ensure the mobile number linked to the UAN is the same as the one linked to Aadhaar for OTP-based e-Sign authentication.
*   **Nominee Details:** Keep the nominee’s Aadhaar number, date of birth, address, and a digital photograph (JPG/JPEG format, size between 50KB to 200KB) ready.

## Phase 2: Execution Steps (Step-by-Step)
*   **Login to Portal:** Navigate to the [EPFO Unified Member Portal](https://unifiedportal-mem.epfindia.gov.in/memberinterface/).
*   **Authentication:** Log in using UAN, password, and the displayed CAPTCHA.
*   **Navigate to e-Nomination:** Click on the 'Manage' tab in the top navigation bar and select 'E-Nomination' from the dropdown menu.
*   **Update Profile:** If prompted to update 'Profile Details' (Address/Photo), complete these fields first and save changes.
*   **Nomination Entry:** 
    *   Click 'Yes' under the 'Have Family?' section to declare family details.
    *   Enter nominee details: Aadhaar, Name, DOB, Gender, Relationship, and Address.
    *   Upload the nominee’s photograph using the 'Upload Photograph' button.
*   **Declaration of Shares:** Enter the percentage of the share to be allocated to the nominee (Total must equal 100%).
*   **Save Details:** Click 'Save EPF Nomination' to generate a temporary record.
*   **E-Sign Authentication:** 
    *   Click on the 'E-Sign' button located under the 'Pending Nomination' tab.
    *   Enter the Aadhaar-linked mobile number to receive the OTP.
    *   Submit the OTP to digitally sign the document. 

## Phase 3: Final Verification
*   **Confirmation:** Upon successful e-Sign, the status will change to 'Nomination Successful'.
*   **Download:** Download the PDF copy of the e-Nomination form for personal records. No physical submission to the employer or EPFO office is required.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls
*   **Pro Tip:** If the member has a minor nominee, ensure a guardian is appointed during the entry phase.
*   **Pro Tip:** Always use a clear, high-resolution photo for the nominee to avoid rejection due to poor image quality.
*   **Pitfall - Mismatched Data:** If the name on the Aadhaar card does not match the EPFO records exactly, the e-Sign will fail. Update EPFO records via the 'Modify Basic Details' request before attempting e-Nomination.
*   **Pitfall - Browser Cache:** Use an incognito window or clear your browser cache if the portal fails to load the OTP submission page after clicking 'E-Sign'.
*   **Pitfall - Expired Aadhaar OTP:** The OTP generated for e-Sign is valid for a limited time (usually 10 minutes). Keep your mobile phone handy during the final step.

## FAQ: Frequently Asked Questions

**Q1: Can I change my nominee after the e-Nomination is submitted?**
Yes, you can file a new e-Nomination at any time. The latest submitted nomination automatically supersedes and cancels all previous nominations.

**Q2: Is it mandatory to provide the nominee's Aadhaar number?**
Yes, providing the nominee's Aadhaar number is mandatory as it acts as the primary identity verification for the nominee within the EPFO ecosystem.

**Q3: What should I do if the e-Sign authentication keeps failing?**
Check if your Aadhaar-linked mobile number is active. If the issue persists, visit the UIDAI website to verify that your Aadhaar demographic details are current, or clear your browser history and try during off-peak hours (early morning or late night).
