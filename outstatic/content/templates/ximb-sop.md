# Standard Operating Procedure: XIMB (Xavier Institute of Management, Bhubaneswar) Operational Workflow

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the standardized operational framework for managing internal academic and administrative processes within XIMB. This document is designed to ensure consistency, accountability, and operational efficiency across faculty, administrative staff, and student-led committees. Adherence to these protocols is mandatory for maintaining the institutional standards of excellence.

## Phase 1: Pre-Operational Planning and Documentation

*   **Requirement Identification:** Define the scope of the project or event. Ensure all resource requests (logistics, IT, finance) are documented in the centralized tracking system.
*   **Stakeholder Alignment:** Secure approval from the relevant Departmental Head or Faculty Coordinator. Ensure all key personnel are informed of the timeline via the official communication channel.
*   **Resource Allocation:** Audit available resources, including classroom bookings, AV equipment, and budgetary provisions. 
*   **Budget Verification:** Cross-reference requested funds against the approved fiscal allocation. Ensure all requisitions are signed by the authorized signatory.

## Phase 2: Execution and Monitoring

*   **Process Initiation:** Execute the planned task according to the predefined SOP timeline. Maintain a real-time log of progress to track key performance indicators (KPIs).
*   **Communication Flow:** All official correspondence must be routed through the established organizational hierarchy to ensure transparency and accountability.
*   **Risk Mitigation:** Monitor for operational bottlenecks (e.g., equipment failure, venue unavailability). Implement contingency plans immediately if a deviation of >10% from the timeline occurs.
*   **Quality Control:** Conduct intermittent spot checks to ensure that the output meets institutional branding and quality standards.

## Phase 3: Post-Operational Review and Archiving

*   **Completion Report:** Compile a comprehensive post-event report summarizing outcomes, budget utilization, and any unforeseen challenges encountered.
*   **Documentation Archiving:** Upload all relevant digital files, receipts, and attendance logs to the central repository for future audits and knowledge management.
*   **Feedback Loop:** Conduct a brief post-mortem meeting with the primary stakeholders to identify "lessons learned" and areas for process optimization.
*   **Final Sign-off:** Obtain formal closure approval from the designated department head to release any remaining encumbered funds or resources.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls

*   **Pro Tip:** Utilize the centralized digital calendar for all room bookings to avoid scheduling conflicts; always book at least 72 hours in advance.
*   **Pro Tip:** For student-run events, maintain a "Handover Document" that includes contact details for vendors and internal IT support to ensure continuity for the following year.
*   **Pitfall:** Lack of documentation. Failing to log petty cash expenses or verbal approvals can lead to significant audit delays.
*   **Pitfall:** Silo-working. Do not initiate procurement processes without notifying the administrative office, as decentralized purchasing often bypasses negotiated institutional discounts.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. What is the turnaround time for a standard resource requisition request?**
Typically, requests should be processed within 3 to 5 business days, provided all supporting documentation and signatures are complete upon submission.

**2. Where can I find the official templates for event proposals and budget tracking?**
These templates are available on the internal faculty/staff portal under the "Operational Resources" tab.

**3. Who is the primary point of contact if an emergency occurs during an event?**
Please contact the Campus Operations Manager or the designated security lead, whose contact information is listed in the Campus Emergency Protocol manual provided during orientation.
