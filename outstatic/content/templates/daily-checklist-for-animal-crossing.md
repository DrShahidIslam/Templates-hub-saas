# Standard Operating Procedure: Daily Island Maintenance (Animal Crossing)

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the daily operational requirements for maintaining an efficient, resource-optimized, and aesthetically pleasing island environment. By following this structured daily workflow, the Island Representative ensures maximum resource extraction, optimal resident satisfaction, and consistent progress toward long-term development objectives. Adherence to this checklist minimizes missed opportunities and maximizes the daily yield of bells, Nook Miles, and rare materials.

## Phase 1: Infrastructure and Resource Extraction

*   **Financial Optimization:** Locate the glowing spot on the ground. Dig it up, bury 10,000 Bells in the hole to produce a Bell Tree.
*   **Mineral Extraction:** Identify the "money rock" and strike all island rocks (typically six) using a shovel, ensuring a buffer of holes behind the player to maintain position for maximum yields (8 items per rock).
*   **Material Harvest:** Shake all non-fruit trees to harvest furniture items, wasp nests, and tree branches. *Note: Ensure a net is equipped prior to shaking to capture wasps.*
*   **Fossil Recovery:** Locate and excavate all buried fossils (typically four to five per day). Assess them at the museum for donation or resale.

## Phase 2: Resident Management and Social Operations

*   **NPC Engagement:** Locate and interact with any visiting NPCs (e.g., Celeste, Sahara, Gulliver, or Leif) to trigger specialized quests or purchase exclusive inventory.
*   **Resident Rapport:** Conduct daily wellness checks with all residents. Provide gifts to high-priority villagers to increase friendship levels and trigger photograph rewards.
*   **Message in a Bottle:** Inspect the shoreline for a Message in a Bottle. Retrieve the DIY recipe contained within to expand the crafting catalog.
*   **Mail Review:** Access the mailbox to collect ordered items, seasonal gifts, or correspondence from residents.

## Phase 3: Administrative and Logistical Tasks

*   **Nook Stop Terminal:** Access the Nook Stop terminal in Resident Services to claim daily Nook Miles login bonuses (maintaining the streak) and check the "Nook Shopping" catalog for daily rotating items.
*   **Shop Inventory Audit:** Visit Nook’s Cranny and the Able Sisters shop to review daily rotating stock, seasonal items, and hot-ticket crafting materials.
*   **Island Evaluation:** Speak with Isabelle to receive the current island evaluation rating. Identify and address specific environmental bottlenecks (e.g., excessive item clutter or lack of decorative variety).

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls

*   **Pitfall - The Inventory Trap:** Always clear your inventory before starting the daily rounds. A cluttered pack leads to inefficient movement and missed resources.
*   **Pro Tip - The Fence Method:** When farming rocks, place two fences or dig two holes diagonally behind your character. This prevents the character from recoiling backward, allowing you to hit the rock the maximum number of times without interruption.
*   **Pitfall - The Wasp Oversight:** Always carry your net while shaking trees. If you are unprepared, you will be stung, forcing you to purchase medicine or wait for a villager to provide a remedy.
*   **Pro Tip - Turnip Logistics:** If you are actively participating in the Stalk Market, check turnip prices at Nook’s Cranny twice daily (before and after noon) to identify the peak selling window.

## FAQ

**Q: Does the "Money Rock" change locations daily?**
A: Yes. The money rock is randomized every day, though it will always be one of the six rocks on your island.

**Q: What happens if I miss a day of Nook Miles login?**
A: Your Nook Miles streak will reset to the baseline (Day 1). This results in a loss of the higher-tier daily bonuses, so consistent daily logins are highly recommended for maximizing mileage.

**Q: Are there benefits to digging up flowers?**
A: Digging up and replanting flowers is necessary if they are overgrowing. Left unchecked, flowers can spread rapidly, causing island performance lag and impacting your Star Rating due to "clutter" metrics.
