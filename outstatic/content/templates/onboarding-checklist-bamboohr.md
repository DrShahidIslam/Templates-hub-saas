# Standard Operating Procedure: New Hire Onboarding via BambooHR

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the standardized process for onboarding new employees using BambooHR. The objective is to ensure a seamless, professional, and compliant transition for every new hire, leveraging BambooHR’s automated workflows to reduce administrative burden and enhance the employee experience. By following this protocol, People Operations teams can ensure that data integrity is maintained, access rights are provisioned timely, and the new hire feels welcomed and prepared from day one.

## Phase 1: Pre-Onboarding & Profile Setup
*   **Initiate Hiring Workflow:** Convert the selected candidate from "Applicant" to "Employee" within the BambooHR ATS to trigger the employee profile creation.
*   **Verify Personal Data:** Ensure accuracy of the legal name, contact information, and primary mailing address.
*   **Assign Employment Status:** Confirm the correct status (Full-time, Part-time, Contractor) to ensure BambooHR applies the correct benefits and pay cycle settings.
*   **Set Compensation Details:** Enter salary, hourly rate, and bonus structures, ensuring alignment with the finalized offer letter.
*   **Define Reporting Line:** Assign the correct Manager and/or Department head to the employee profile to trigger correct approval workflows.

## Phase 2: Workflow & Task Automation
*   **Trigger Onboarding Template:** Select the appropriate "Onboarding Template" (e.g., Remote, On-site, or Executive) to auto-populate the new hire’s dashboard.
*   **Document Distribution:** Utilize the "Electronic Signature" feature to send tax forms (W-4/I-9), the Employee Handbook, and the Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA).
*   **Assign Training Modules:** Ensure mandatory compliance training links are embedded in the employee’s onboarding checklist.
*   **Provisioning Requests:** Use BambooHR task assignments to notify IT of hardware needs and security access requirements.

## Phase 3: Welcome & Integration
*   **Send Welcome Email:** Customize the automated welcome email sent via BambooHR, including login instructions and the "First Day" agenda.
*   **Add to Company Directory:** Ensure the new hire’s photo and bio are uploaded to the Employee Directory to encourage peer recognition.
*   **Manager Notification:** Verify that the "New Hire Announcement" is scheduled for the correct date to inform the broader team.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls
*   **Pro Tip:** Create custom "Smart Groups" in BambooHR. This allows you to automatically filter onboarding tasks based on location or department, ensuring local laws (e.g., state-specific tax forms) are respected.
*   **Pro Tip:** Use the "Audit Trail" feature in BambooHR to track exactly when an employee views or signs a document, which is critical for compliance reporting.
*   **Pitfall:** Overloading the new hire. Do not assign all documents and tasks to the first day. Spread "Read & Sign" tasks over the first week to prevent information fatigue.
*   **Pitfall:** Forgetting to "Archive" incomplete onboarding tasks. If a document remains unsigned, set a recurring automated reminder in BambooHR rather than relying on manual follow-ups.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**1. How do I change an onboarding task once it has already been assigned?**
If you need to edit a task, navigate to the employee’s "Onboarding" tab, locate the specific task, and click "Edit" or "Remove." Note that if the employee has already started the task, you may need to reset it for them to see the changes.

**2. Can I track the progress of my onboarding workflow?**
Yes. Use the "Onboarding Reports" section in BambooHR. You can pull a report on all active employees in the onboarding phase to see completion percentages and identify where bottlenecks occur.

**3. What should I do if a new hire is not receiving their system emails?**
First, verify that the email address in their profile is correct. Next, check the "Email Logs" in the BambooHR settings menu to see if the email was sent, bounced, or blocked by the recipient's spam filter.
