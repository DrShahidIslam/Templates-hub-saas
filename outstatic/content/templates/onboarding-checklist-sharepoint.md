# Standard Operating Procedure: Employee Onboarding via SharePoint

This Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) outlines the standardized process for utilizing SharePoint as the primary hub for employee onboarding. By centralizing documentation, task management, and resource access, this procedure ensures a consistent, professional, and audit-ready experience for all new hires. The following checklist is designed to facilitate seamless cross-departmental collaboration and ensure that every new team member has the information they need from Day One.

## Phase 1: Preparation & Access Provisioning
*   **Create Site Folder:** Generate a dedicated folder within the 'Onboarding' SharePoint library named `[LastName]_[FirstName]_Onboarding`.
*   **Grant Permissions:** Configure 'Read/Write' access for the new hire and 'Owner' access for the hiring manager and HR representative.
*   **Deploy Welcome Dashboard:** Duplicate the 'New Hire Template' page and populate it with the employee’s name and start date.
*   **IT Hardware Verification:** Upload the hardware delivery tracking number to the 'IT Assets' list within the site.
*   **Credential Distribution:** Securely share initial login credentials via the SharePoint ‘Secure Documents’ portal rather than email.

## Phase 2: Documentation & Compliance
*   **Policy Review:** Upload the Employee Handbook, Code of Conduct, and IT Usage Policy to the 'Required Reading' library.
*   **Digital Signatures:** Link the e-signature portal (e.g., DocuSign/Adobe Sign) for employment contracts and NDAs directly on the SharePoint landing page.
*   **Tax Forms:** Ensure Federal and State tax forms are uploaded to the secure 'HR Private' folder.
*   **Confirmation Log:** Update the status tracking list to verify that all mandatory documents have been acknowledged and signed.

## Phase 3: Orientation & Training Modules
*   **The 30-60-90 Plan:** Upload the specific goal-setting document to the 'Growth & Development' folder.
*   **Video Library:** Curate a playlist of recorded company culture and department-specific training sessions.
*   **Org Chart Integration:** Embed the latest departmental organizational chart to provide context for team hierarchy.
*   **Calendar Sync:** Upload a draft of the onboarding week schedule, including key stakeholder "meet-and-greets."

## Phase 4: Final Verification & Access Review
*   **Permissions Audit:** Perform a final check to ensure the new hire can access all folders/links required for their role.
*   **Link Integrity Check:** Click every hyperlink on the dashboard to ensure there are no '404' or 'Access Denied' errors.
*   **Manager Handoff:** Send a final notification email to the hiring manager confirming the portal is live and ready for the employee’s Day One.

## Pro Tips & Pitfalls
*   **Pro Tip:** Use the 'Versioning' feature in SharePoint document libraries to ensure the new hire always sees the most recent draft of onboarding documents.
*   **Pro Tip:** Create a 'New Hire FAQ' list where employees can submit questions; this prevents the same questions from being asked repeatedly via email.
*   **Pitfall:** Do not use granular, folder-level permissions if possible; it creates a "permission hell" scenario that is difficult to audit. Use SharePoint groups instead.
*   **Pitfall:** Avoid over-populating the landing page. Keep it clean with clear headings so new hires do not feel overwhelmed by information density.

## Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

**Q: Should I grant the new hire full site administration rights?**
A: No. Grant the new hire 'Read' access to general information and 'Contribute' access only to their specific personal onboarding folders. Full administration rights should remain with HR/IT.

**Q: How do I handle sensitive documents (e.g., social security numbers)?**
A: All highly sensitive documents must be stored in a SharePoint library with 'Restricted Access' and 'Information Rights Management' (IRM) enabled to prevent unauthorized downloading or printing.

**Q: Can I automate the onboarding process within SharePoint?**
A: Yes. Use 'Power Automate' to trigger email notifications to the manager whenever the new hire marks an onboarding task as 'Complete' on their dashboard.
